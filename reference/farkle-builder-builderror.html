BuildError.SymbolLimit
Signature: int

The maximum number of terminals and nonterminals a grammar built by Farkle can have. Currently set to 2^20; sixteen times more of what GOLD Parser can handle. To be more specific, a grammar can have at most 2^20 terminals and 2^20 nonterminals.
